Research

My research explores the intersection of law, medicine, and madness in late imperial China, with particular interests in legal medicine, and the history of the body. I am currently developing a book manuscript titled Legal Justice, Medical Authority, and Madness in China, 1600–1930. My broader research agenda extends to comparative Euro-Asian histories of law and medicine, as well as the study of Manchu-language sources.
