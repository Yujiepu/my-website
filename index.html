Yujie (Elena) Pu

Assistant Professor of History

Specializing in Legal History, Medicine, and Madness in Late Imperial China

Smith College Department of History

I am an Assistant Professor of History at Smith College. My research explores the intersection of law, medicine, and madness in late imperial China, with particular interests in legal medicine and the history of the body. I am currently completing a book manuscript titled Legal Justice, Medical Authority, and Madness in China, 1600-1930.
